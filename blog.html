So what's an Undertow anyway? - 4/30/17

un·der·tow

another term for rip current, used in the incorrect belief that rip currents drag swimmers below the surface.

an implicit quality, emotion, or influence underlying the superficial aspects of something and leaving a particular impression.

Undertow is a game I've been thinking of making since around 2013. The whole idea actually came from one of the powers in the game BioShock: Infinite (which unfortunately ended up dissapointing me as a game). Nevertheless, this unique power in the game let you push and pull your foes using a beam of water. I thought this mechanic was super cool, but it also didn't really change the game in any way. Ultimately, it was just a cool power-up.
