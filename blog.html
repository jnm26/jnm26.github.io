So what's an Undertow anyway? - 4/30/17

un·der·tow

another term for rip current, used in the incorrect belief that rip currents drag swimmers below the surface.

an implicit quality, emotion, or influence underlying the superficial aspects of something and leaving a particular impression.

Undertow is a game I've been thinking of making since around 2013. The whole idea actually came from one of the powers in the game BioShock: Infinite (which unfortunately ended up dissapointing me as a game). It's a 2D game which focuses on player movement, and having a wide array of options to approach any given situation, primarily using water-based abilities.
