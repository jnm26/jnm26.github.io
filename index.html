About John Moreira

Thanks for coming to my site! I'm a programmer with interest in game dev, optimization, and hardware/software interfacing.

I started programming when I was a freshman in High School when I took a Visual Basic/C# class. I had loved computers and computer games since I was a kid, so learning programming was the natural progression of my interests. I continued through all of the programming courses my School Offered, and it wasn't long before I decided that Computer Science and programming was my passion. I loved the freedom, utility, and opportunity presented by the medium. Since College, I've participated in quite a few Hackathons and Game Jams, working with my friends on various projects that interested us.
